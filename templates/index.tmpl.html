The Pledge


 I pledge to work together, not against, my peers. I will help my fellow classmates understand the material and ask for help when necessary. I will be respectful of others that struggle with the content by creating a safe, non-judgmental learning environment.

 I see the importance of an education as more than just passing a class, but understanding the material. I pledge to stand up against the traditional, competitive atmosphere of a college campus.

Tips

  1. Ask questions in class.
  2. Attend office hours.
  3. Make study groups. Reach out to your classmates in the beginning of the quarter and try to make a fb group or groupchat.
  4. Ask for help. If you're lost don't stay silent and fall farther behind.
  5. Checkin on Study Space to see who else is studying.
 

Study Space

At Study Space we have always believed that the current atmosphere on college campuses is fundamentally flawed. We created the Collaborative Campus Initiative (CCI) to take the first step in fixing the issues we see. We hope that every student at UCD will work together for the betterment of our educations, instead of for personal gain.

Study Space is an educational platform that help students find places to study and students to study with.

We are made by students for students and hope to connect every student at UCD with the tools they need to succeed. Join the beta to get started and become eligible for awesome prizes.



When Karissa, Lynn, and I started Study Space last spring, we had a vision to make a real impact on the way students study. I truly hope that this initiative can be a starting point for real change across college campuses. At it's current state, university classrooms feel more like battlegrounds than places of learning.

Please take the time to sign the pledge. It's the first step towards making a difference.

- Jacob Bevilacqua, CEO
