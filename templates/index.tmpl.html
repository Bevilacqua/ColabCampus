Collaborative Campus Initiative

The Pledge


 I pledge to work together, not against, my peers. I'll help my fellow classmates understand the material and ask for help when necessary. I'll be respectful of others that struggle with the content by creating a safe, non-judgmental learning environment.

 I see the importance of an education as more than just passing a class, but understanding the material. I pledge to stand up against the traditional, competitive atmosphere of a college campus.
